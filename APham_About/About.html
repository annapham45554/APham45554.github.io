About Anna

Background

Anna was born on October 16, 2001, in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. She is the youngest sister in a quadruplet. Growing up, she had always wanted to be an artist who specializes in fine arts and modern arts. She realized that the artist career is not stable. After doing some research, she found design as the best career option for financial stability and creativity.


Education

Anna is currently pursuing an SFSU Visual Communication Design major, she blends creativity with functionality to craft visually appealing, accessible, and user-friendly designs. She is eager to contribute her skills to innovative projects and collaborate with like-minded professionals.


Relationship

Anna has been in a 2.5-year committed relationship, she is living with her partner in the Bay Area and sharing every moment with him. He was the one who inspired her to get into the design field. Together, they continue to learn from each other as they get to know each other more.


Anna is engaged and looking forward to getting married! She is nervous yet excited as she step into the new chapter of her life.
